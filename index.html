Why we Exist

Nowadays, there are countless of local beef with low nutritional value due to the scarcity of high-nutritions feed that were given to them, especially in Indonesia. Forage-worthy grass is getting more and more difficult to find due to land conversion and climate change. Farmers tend to bite their finger since cattle feed price is inflating while their cattle quality keeps on declining. Hereby NITYA comes with a mission. We thrive to solve this problem by offering affordable cattle feed with higher nutritional value by utilizing the treasure of our maritime nation, which is Seaweed. Our goal is to escalate the quality of our local beef with cheaper prices. We also provide cattle farming workshops and vet visits in one package of membership.
